--- name: getavalanchekey version: 0.21.13 group: avalanche permalink: doc/0.21.13/rpc/avalanche/getavalanchekey/ --- getavalanchekey Returns the key used to sign avalanche messages. Examples: > curl --user myusername --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "getavalanchekey", "params": [] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;' http://127.0.0.1:8332/
