--- layout: base title: Electrum ABC ---

This wallet software is forked from Electron Cash, with modifications to facilitate use on the BCHA network.

Upon first use, the software will automatically copy existing Electron Cash wallet files into a new folder specific to Electrum ABC. This allows users to maintain separate wallet histories for either network going forward, and run Electron Cash and Electrum ABC simultaneously to ease coin splitting.

NOTE: The coins in the wallet will not split automatically. To ensure that transactions are not replayed between BCH (BCHN) and BCHA networks, please refer to one of the coin splitting guides linked at bitcoinabc.org/bcha and make sure your pre-fork coins are safely split prior to transacting on either network (BCH or BCHA).
